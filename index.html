Introduction

Nunito Sans is the primary typeface for Drip Capital. It evokes a contemporary, clean and balanced visual identity. Along with being modern and minimalist in appearance, the use of Bold and Regular from this font family provides high-legibility.

Heading Structure

To enhance our design language consistently across all digital products, our curated font sizes range from 44px to 10px to establish harmonious visual hierarchy.

Usage Examples

ℹ️ Load the component once, then use <drip-text> anywhere in your HTML. 
<!-- 1. Import the web component (once per page) -->
<script type="module" src="./src/components/DripText.js"></script>

<!-- 2. Import design tokens (once per page) -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="./src/tokens/typography.css" />

<!-- Desktop headings -->
<drip-text variant="h1">The seamless trade platform</drip-text>
<drip-text variant="h2" weight="bold">cashflow needs</drip-text>
<drip-text variant="caption">Developed with precision</drip-text>

<!-- Body & utility -->
<drip-text variant="body">Nunito Sans is the primary typeface for Drip Capital.</drip-text>
<drip-text variant="label" weight="bold">assisted for you</drip-text>
<drip-text variant="validation">to global trade finance</drip-text>

<!-- Mobile scale (add the `mobile` attribute) -->
<drip-text variant="h1" mobile>The seamless trade platform</drip-text>

<!-- Custom colour override -->
<drip-text variant="h3" color="#1e7a4e">Custom colour</drip-text>

<!-- Render as a different tag -->
<drip-text variant="h2" as="span">Inline heading</drip-text>
